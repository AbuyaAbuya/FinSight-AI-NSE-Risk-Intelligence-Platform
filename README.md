## Overview

FinSight AI is a Machine Learning-powered financial intelligence platform designed to forecast stock volatility, assess investment risk, and support data-driven decision-making.

The platform integrates stock market analytics, volatility forecasting, portfolio risk simulation, executive dashboards, AI-powered investment recommendations, and board-level reporting into a unified financial intelligence solution.

## Key Features

### 📊 Market Explorer

Interactive analysis of NSE-listed companies including share price trends, trading activity, daily returns, and market performance.

### 📈 Volatility Forecasting

Machine Learning-based volatility estimation using engineered market indicators and historical trading patterns.

### 🤖 Model Performance Analytics

Evaluation of forecasting accuracy using RMSE, MAE, and R² performance metrics.

### 📑 Research Findings

Presentation of key findings, model insights, business implications, and executive recommendations.

### 🏢 Executive Dashboard

Executive-level risk monitoring dashboard featuring market risk scores, investment recommendations, and portfolio intelligence.

### 💡 AI Advisory Center

AI-generated investment recommendations based on stock risk classifications and market conditions.

### 💼 Portfolio Risk Simulator

Interactive portfolio construction and risk assessment environment for evaluating allocation strategies.

### 📋 Board Reporting Pack

Board-ready reporting interface summarizing market conditions, portfolio exposure, and investment risk.
