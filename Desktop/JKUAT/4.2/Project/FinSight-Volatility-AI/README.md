# 📈 FinSight AI NSE Risk Intelligence Platform

Executive Market Risk Intelligence Platform for Companies Listed on the Nairobi Securities Exchange (NSE).

---

## Overview

FinSight AI is a Machine Learning-powered financial intelligence platform designed to forecast stock volatility, assess investment risk, and support data-driven decision-making.

The platform combines market analytics, volatility forecasting, risk scoring, portfolio simulation, executive dashboards, AI-powered investment recommendations, and board-level reporting into a unified financial intelligence solution.

---

## Key Features

### 📊 Market Explorer
Interactive exploration of NSE-listed companies, including share price trends, returns analysis, trading activity, and market performance.

### 📈 Volatility Forecasting
Machine Learning-based volatility estimation and forecasting using engineered market features and rolling volatility indicators.

### 🤖 Model Performance Analytics
Evaluation of forecasting accuracy using industry-standard performance metrics including RMSE, MAE, and R².

### 📑 Research Findings Dashboard
Presentation of key analytical findings, model insights, and business implications derived from the study.

### 🏢 Executive Dashboard
Executive-level market intelligence dashboard featuring risk scoring, investment recommendations, and market monitoring indicators.

### 💡 AI Advisory Center
AI-generated investment guidance based on market risk levels and stock volatility classifications.

### 💼 Portfolio Risk Simulator
Interactive portfolio construction and risk assessment tool for evaluating investment allocations and portfolio-level risk exposure.

### 📋 Board Reporting Pack
Board-ready reporting interface summarizing risk exposure, market conditions, and strategic investment insights.
